Pineapple Pizza

Pineapple pizza combines the perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors in a simple yet irresistible recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced Canadian bacon or ham
  • Pineapple chunks (fresh or canned)
  • Olivr oil (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll or stretch the pizza dough into your desired shape on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce.
  5. Arrange slices of Canadian bacon or ham evenly across the pizza.
  6. Add pineapple chunks on top, spacing them out for balanced flavor.
  7. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil over the toppings (optional).
  8. Bake the pizza for 10–12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two.
  10. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes if desired, slice, and serve hot.
