Lasagna

Lasagna is a hearty, layered Italian dish that combines rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and tender pasta sheets for the ultimate comfort food experience.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Olive oil
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Egg (for ricotta mixture)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and cook the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned, draining excess fat.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 15–20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and a bit of Parmesan; mix until smooth.
  6. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions if not using oven-ready noodles.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Add a layer of noodles, followed by ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake an additional 15–20 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  11. Let rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing.
  12. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley and serve warm.
