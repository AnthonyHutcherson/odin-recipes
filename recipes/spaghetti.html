Spaghetti

Spghetti

Spaghetti is a timeless Italian classic that blends tender pasta with a rich, flavorful tomato sauce for a satisfying and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage (optional for meat sauce)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning or dried oregano and basil
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add spaghetti noodles and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  4. Sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until soft and fragrant.
  5. Add ground beef or sausage (if using) and cook until browned; drain excess fat.
  6. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  7. Add a small pinch of sugar to balance the sauce’s acidity (optional).
  8. Simmer the sauce for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally to thicken and deepen the flavor.
  9. Add the cooked spaghetti into the sauce and toss until evenly coated.
  10. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil or parsley for garnish.
